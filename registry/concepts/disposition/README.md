# `disposition` — Concept seed

The referential-domain Concept. Names *what subject matter* a
Trope, an authored instance, or a session is about. `base` is
closed at three values (`Product | Protocol | Infrastructure`);
optional `scope`, `prompt`, `prose` are open freeform fields.
Orthogonal to Mode (operational stance); the two axes form the
2-D product space pattern-matched at every authoritative
branching site.
