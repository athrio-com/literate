A *decision* is an append-only record of a structural or
architectural choice. The reasoning chain is preserved; only the
`Status` line is mutable, recording supersession or deferral over
time.

This page assembles the `decisions` Concept (shape and
invariants), the `decisions` Trope (LF's canonical realisation),
the **ADR** subkind (the v0.1 default — Architecture Decision
Record), and the `adr-flow` workflow Trope (the steps for
drafting, gating, and landing an ADR).
