# Person

The collaborator directing work. The Person authors decisions, gates
reviewed prose at the review gate (Accept / Correct / Clarify /
Reject), and owns all output of an LF session — both prose and code.
The Person holds authorial responsibility; the AI drafts and derives
under the Person's direction.

The capitalisation distinguishes the role from the generic English
word. A particular human is *a* person; in LF prose, references to
"the Person" always mean the role acting on the current session.
