# AI

The language-model collaborator. The AI drafts prose under the
Person's direction, surfaces inconsistencies across the corpus, and
derives code from prose that the Person has accepted. The AI does
not decide; it drafts, presents, and executes.

Capitalised to name the role, not a specific model. The same role is
played by whichever model family a session is run with; session logs
record the specific model as `Agent:`.
