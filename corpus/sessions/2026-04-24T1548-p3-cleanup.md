### Discovered in this session

- **`Data.TaggedError` + `override get message()`** is the
  clean pattern for tagged errors that want a formatted
  multi-line message. `Data.TaggedError` supplies `_tag`, name,
  and instance-of semantics; the `override get message()`
  recomputes from `this.<fields>` each access. Caller code
  written against `instanceof ProseSchemaViolations` +
  `.violations` keeps working unchanged; the sole change is the
  constructor shape (`new X({ violations })` vs. `new X(violations)`).
- **Pre-existing-drift sweep policy**. When a Person-flagged
  cleanup surfaces drift that predates the flagged session, the
  right move is to surface the precise origin (commit +
  session) and recommend, rather than silently extend scope.
  Recorded here as an implicit feedback for similar future
  sessions: *cleanup scope = what the flagged session
  introduced*, plus anything the Person explicitly directs on
  surfacing.
- **The Effect-idiom split in `@literate/cli`** is real and
  structural: `verbs/continue.ts` + `verbs/close.ts` use
  `Effect.gen` + Layer composition because they dispatch the
  Step substrate from `@literate/core`; every other verb and
  every service under `src/registry/*` + `src/weaver/*` is
  plain async/await. The split is load-bearing only as
  historical-path artefact; the `cli-effect-and-bun-only`
  successor eliminates it.
