## Summary

Established the v0.1 Literate Framework scaffold and then
reshaped it twice mid-session toward what was actually discussed
in the migration conversation. The repo now ships LF as 19
typed npm-publishable workspace packages (7 Concept packages, 10
Trope packages, the core library, the CLI, and the minimal
template), with cross-Trope references resolved by the
TypeScript compiler instead of a string registry. The CLI walks
the typed graph and materialises `.literate/` for consumers; the
docs site reads MDX from `packages/*` and presents one composed
page per domain (corpus, sessions, decisions, categories,
concepts, tropes, chapters) plus a flat sidebar. The `Term`
primitive was unified into `Concept` (one primitive, two scopes
— LF-level packages and corpus-level files); the corresponding
ADR-010 supersedes ADR-006. Ten ADRs are accepted; the corpus,
LF-product, and docs-site faces of the repo are mutually
consistent and verifiable end-to-end (`bun install`, `tsc
--noEmit`, `next build`, and the `literate init / check` smoke
test all pass).
