A *session* is a bounded work unit with a log that records its
Goals, decisions, work done, and summary. Sessions are LF's
operational heartbeat: work happens inside a session, gated Goals
scope what is authored, and a session-end validation step catches
drift before it accumulates.

This page assembles everything you need to run sessions in LF: the
`session` Concept (the shape of a session log), the `session`
Trope (LF's canonical realisation), the `session-start` and
`session-end` workflow Tropes (how a session begins and closes),
and the `goal-flow` Trope (how Goals are drafted, gated, and
retired).
