# Session

A session is a bounded work unit with a log that records its Goals,
decisions, work done, and summary. Sessions are LF's operational
heartbeat: work happens inside a session, gated Goals scope what is
authored, and session-end validation catches drift before it
accumulates.

Each session has a log file at
`corpus/sessions/YYYY-MM-DDTHHMM-<slug>.md`. The body is append-once;
the `## Goals` and `## Plan` sections are gated (each entry passes
the review gate); the `## Summary` is written once at session end.

A session has a Status drawn from the closed set in
[`corpus/categories/session-status.md`](../../../../corpus/categories/session-status.md):
`Planned`, `Open`, `Closed`, `Abandoned`. The default lifecycle is
`Open → Closed`; the optional planning lifecycle is
`Planned → Open → Closed`. Either lifecycle may end at `Abandoned`
under explicit Person disposition.

## Log shape

Header:

- `Date` — `YYYY-MM-DD`. For a `Planned` session this is the
  intended start date; for an `Open` or `Closed` session this is
  the actual start date.
- `Started` — `YYYY-MM-DDTHH:MM`. Absent (or `—`) on a `Planned`
  session that has not yet been started.
- `Status` — one of `Planned`, `Open`,
  `Closed (YYYY-MM-DDTHH:MM)`, `Abandoned`.
- `Chapter` — chapter handle, or `—` if none.
- `Agent` — model identifier (or `—` if not yet bound).
- `Planned by` — optional. Path to the parent session log whose
  `## Plan` named this session.

Sections:

- `## Goals` — session-scope specifications. Each entry declares
  `Topic`, `Upstream`, optional `Scope`, `Out of scope`,
  `Acceptance`, `Notes`. On Accept, `Status` and `Category` are
  added. A `Planned` session may carry provisional Goals adopted
  verbatim from a parent's Plan entry; the successor session
  re-gates them on transition to `Open`.
- `## Plan` — optional. Present on sessions that pre-scope a
  multi-session arc. The Plan declares an ordered sequence of
  provisional successor sessions; each entry carries `Slug`,
  `Topic`, `Depends on` (optional), and a Goals list whose
  entries carry `Topic`, `Upstream`, `Acceptance`. Each Plan
  entry is gated. Once a successor session transitions to `Open`,
  its Plan entry is frozen and gains a `Realised by` field
  pointing to the successor's log.
- `## Decisions Made` — references to ADRs and Concept-level
  material revisions accepted during the session.
- `## Work Done` — files created / modified / deleted with
  rationale.
- `## Summary` — 2–4 sentences written at session end.
- `## Deferred / Discovered` — optional carry-over items. At
  `Closed`, any unrealised Plan entries are referenced here.

## Lifecycle

### Spontaneous (default)

1. Session begins; the `session-start` Trope executes;
   `Status: Open`.
2. Goal(s) drafted; gated; on Accept work proceeds.
3. ADRs / Concepts / specs / chapters drafted and gated as work
   demands.
4. Session ends; the `session-end` Trope executes; `Summary` is
   written; `Status` is stamped `Closed (timestamp)`.

### Planned

1. A parent session authors a `## Plan` block; each Plan entry is
   gated. On Accept, a successor log file is created at
   `corpus/sessions/YYYY-MM-DDTHHMM-<slug>.md` with `Status:
   Planned` and the entry's provisional Goals copied in. The
   parent's Plan entry references the successor by path.
2. The successor remains `Planned` until work begins. While
   `Planned`, its Goals may be revised; revisions are gated.
3. When work begins, `session-start` reads the `Planned` log,
   stamps `Status: Open` and `Started: YYYY-MM-DDTHH:MM`,
   re-presents the Goals for re-gating (the Person may Accept
   verbatim, Correct, Clarify, or Reject any Goal), and the
   parent's Plan entry is frozen.
4. From here the lifecycle proceeds as the spontaneous case.

A `Planned` session may transition directly to `Abandoned` if
the Person decides not to execute it; the parent's Plan entry
is annotated accordingly.

## Mutability

- Body outside `## Goals` and `## Plan` lands without a gate.
- `## Goals` entries are gated at first-author and on material
  revision. Status transitions (`Active` → `Completed` /
  `Superseded by Goal N`) are atomic and ungated; `Abandoned`
  is gated.
- `## Plan` entries are gated at first-author and on material
  revision. While the entry's successor is `Planned`, the entry
  may still be revised; once the successor transitions to
  `Open`, the entry is frozen and only its `Realised by` field
  is set atomically.
- `## Summary` is written once at session end; later edits are
  gated.

## Relationships

- Realised by the `session` Trope.
- Depends on `corpus` (sessions live in `corpus/sessions/`).
- Composed with `session-start`, `session-end`, and `goal-flow`
  workflow Tropes.
