# ADR Subkind

The Architecture Decision Record subkind of the `decisions` Trope.
ADRs capture structural or architectural choices that shape code
and downstream prose.

## File naming

`corpus/decisions/ADR-NNN-<slug>.md` — zero-padded three-digit
number; lowercase hyphenated slug derived from the title.

## Header

```markdown
# ADR-NNN — title

**Date:** YYYY-MM-DD
**Status:** Open | Accepted | Deferred | Superseded by ADR-NNN
**Tags:** `#tag-a` `#tag-b`
```

## Body sections

- `Context:` — why the decision was needed.
- `Decision:` — what was chosen.
- `Consequences:` — what changes as a result.
- `Deferred:` (optional) — narrowed scope for follow-up.

## Authorial discipline

ADRs are narrative. They record the reasoning chain. An ADR a year
later reads as an explanation to a reader who was not present when
it was decided.

Append-only once Accepted. The `Status:` line is the sole mutable
part for supersession or deferral.
